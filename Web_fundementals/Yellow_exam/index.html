Helping others with your computer

A computer is vital tool for anyone living in this day and age. This is why we aim to donate every 1 in 10 computers we collect to charitable and worthy causes.

Safe & Secure

We ensure all previously stored infromation on any of the systems we collect is either removed, given to you or destroyed.

Eco-Friendly

We aim to reuse all that we collected from you. Reducing wastage and the manufacture of new systems has a range of benift for our futures in this plant.

Helping Others

We donate 1 in 10 machines to charities and worthy causes. This helps grow individuals who are in need of the benifits a computer can bring.

Welcome to We Collect Your IT

We offer a free collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse this equipment by ensureing any sensetive or personal information that was on these machines is either physically removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benify the environment by avoiding the waste and disposal of this equipment but shall also help others, as we donate and proportion of all the equipment we have collected.

Why waste ? We will collect....

The environment and social benift of reuse do not only help conserve nartural resources, but help redue air and water pollution caused by hazardous disposal. Reuse reduces the amount of greenhouse gas emissions caused by the manufacturing of new products. It simply makes good sense to let us collect and reuse your equipment.
